Events Held There

Since the George R. Brown Convention Center is not a store, I made this page a gallery of events instead of a product page.

DreamCon

DreamCon is a pop culture event with anime, gaming, comics, creators, and fans.

Comicpalooza

Comicpalooza is a pop culture event with comics, cosplay, guests, panels, and fandom activities.

Anime Matsuri

Anime Matsuri is an anime and Japanese culture convention with cosplay, music, panels, and vendors.

Collect-A-Con

Collect-A-Con is for people who like trading cards, anime, gaming, toys, and collectibles.

Houston Fishing Show

The Houston Fishing Show has fishing gear, boats, outdoor equipment, vendors, and demonstrations.

Houston Autorama

Houston Autorama is a car show with custom cars, classic cars, trucks, and other vehicle displays.

Detailed Information

The George R. Brown Convention Center, also called the GRB, is one of Houston’s largest event venues. It is used for conventions, exhibitions, meetings, festivals, and large public events.

History

The George R. Brown Convention Center opened on September 26, 1987. It was named after George Rufus Brown, a Houston entrepreneur, engineer, civic leader, and philanthropist.
